Frequently Asked Questions

Last updated: July 9, 2026

What does Nevio actually do?

You photograph a mole or skin spot, and Nevio gives you a plain-language visual assessment (based on the ABCDE criteria dermatologists use for early awareness — Asymmetry, Border, Color, Diameter, Evolution) plus a suggested next step. You can re-photograph the same mole over time to see if anything has visibly changed.

Is this a medical diagnosis?

No. Nevio is a visual awareness tool, not a diagnostic device, and it never provides a medical diagnosis. Every result includes a reminder of this, and anything you're concerned about should be evaluated in person by a dermatologist.

How accurate is the analysis?

The analysis is generated by an AI vision model and, like any automated tool, can be wrong in either direction — it can flag something harmless, or miss something that a trained dermatologist would catch. Treat it as a helpful nudge to pay attention, not a verdict.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your photos are stored in a private cloud bucket with no public link — every view uses a temporary, signed URL generated just for you. We don't sell data or use third-party ad trackers. See our Privacy Policy for the full details.

Can I delete my data?

Yes, any time — Settings → Delete Account permanently erases your account, every photo, and all analysis history. This can't be undone.

Why was my photo rejected?

Nevio rejects photos that either aren't of a mole/skin spot at all, or are too blurry, dark, or far away to assess reliably. Retake the photo in good lighting with the spot clearly in focus and try again.

How do recheck reminders work?

You can turn on a reminder for any tracked mole from its detail screen. Reminders are scheduled entirely on your device — they'll fire even if the app is closed, and no reminder data is sent to our servers.

I signed up with Apple — can I also use a password?

Yes — go to Settings and set a password at any time. Your account stays the same, you'll just have a second way to log in.

I don't see this question — who do I ask?

Email us at support@nevio.agency and we'll get back to you.
